What is HIBM - Neuromuscular Disease Foundation

HIBM is a rare genetic disease that causes muscles to slowly waste away. HIBM is not life-threatening but it may lead to physical debilitation within two decades of diagnosis. Symptoms usually begin to develop in early adulthood, between late teens to early 30's.
